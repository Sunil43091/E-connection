About - Connections 24/7 Support

At Connections Entertainment we offer a 360-degree solution for your event and cater to your specific demands, no matter where in Norfolk you plan to host and how large, small, simple, or imaginative your event is.

We'll provide you with complete confidence and peace of mind throughout the planning process. We collaborate with you every step of the process, from planning to production, as a flexible, professional, and fun-loving team.

What to expect

Our team experience comprises over 30 years of expertise, we've provided world-class support and entertainment on thousands of events, from Weddings and Engagements to Kids Parties, Charity Events and Conferences.

Our team is ready to supply you with the greatest entertainment and events staff, working with local, regional, and national clients and meeting and exceeding your expectations, whether you need a support service, a booking agent, or a whole event management package.

We can arrange to Book and Confirm the services you require on your behalf.

If you can't find the service you are looking for on the Connections Site, our team will endeavour to accommodate your needs by checking internal databases and our partners in Norfolk and all over the country
